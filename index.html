Birds are bountiful in Baltimore City!

Baltimore City and it's surrounding regions are a welcome haven to birds. For many migratory birds, it's waterways and rolling fields lined with brush and forest are an inviting refuge on thier long journey It's many park spaces are year-round homes to generations of woodpeckers and songbirds. Even larger birds like hawks, owls and vultures have settled to call Baltimore's urban landscape home.
